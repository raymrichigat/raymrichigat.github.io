Pantai Tiga Warna, Malang

Pantai Tiga Warna is a protected forest, coral reef, and mangrove restoration and conservation area. With water that is various shades of blue, green, and brown, the Three Color Beach is one of a kind. The depth of the ocean is the reason behind the different hues of the seawater. Brown relates to the beach sand, green is found in the shallow regions, and blue denotes the deepest portion.

Jawa Timur Park 1

One of Malang's most well-known tourist attractions is Jatim Park 1. Teenagers who want to push their boundaries love this place because it's full with adrenaline-pumping attractions. This tourist destination offers everything from bumper cars to frenzy rides to roller coasters. Furthermore, there is a body museum that provides visitors with educational opportunities to learn more about the organs in their own bodies.
