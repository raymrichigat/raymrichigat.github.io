Kuliner Pertama

Mie Bakar Celaket, a Malang delicacy that has gone viral. This is a fusion of European and Javanese cuisine, with local seasonings utilized to make the noodles' spiciness and items like chicken and mozzarella cheese for the dish's presentation and toppings. When visiting Malang, this food is a must-try, especially for young people or millennials.

Kuliner Kedua

Bakso, one of Malang's well-known regional delicacies, is a must-try when visiting the city. This is a gourmet delicacy that is available all throughout the city. Malang meatballs are served with fine meatballs, tendon meatballs, siomay, tofu, and yellow noodles, or so'un. Fried snacks are an example of optional condiments, in addition to the previously stated condiments. As an accompaniment to this lunch, a variety of fried appetizers are available. There are various types of fried treats such as fried intestines, fried lungs, and flower fritters.
